About Us

WINTERVELDT COMMUNITY RADIO NPC is a non-profit community radio station established in 2025 to provide locally-relevant information, entertainment, and educational programming. We engage with community stakeholders and empower citizens through accessible broadcasting.

Our Vision

To be the primary community platform for participatory media, local development, and cultural expression in Winterveldt and adjacent communities.
